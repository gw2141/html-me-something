Garrett's Jalepeno Popper Guide

  • Ingrdients.
  • Jalepenos.
  • Cream Cheese- 2 packs.
  • Bacon.
  • BBQ Rub.

    • Directions.

  • 1. Season Cream Cheese with BBQ Rub.
  • 2. Cut Jalepenos in half and scoop seeds.
  • 3. Fill Jalepenos halves with seasoned Cream Cheese.
  • 4. Wrap filled Jalepeno half with bacon.
  • 5. Smoke Poppers for 1 hour at 300* with Hickory chunks.

    • Notes

    This recipe requires a kettle grill
